About Me

Welcome! I'm a postdoc at UC Santa Barbara, working with Brendan Bowler. I received my PhD in 2025 from UCLA, where I worked with Erik Petigura to explore exoplanet occurrence and orbital characteristics. I am especially interested in combining multiple detection techniques to improve our understanding of exosystem structure.

I am interested in understanding the relationships between different objects that orbit stars. Some are planets, which can be small and rocky, or big and gaseous. Some are close to their host star, while others orbit far away. Some aren't planets at all, and instead fall into the category of "brown dwarfs." Some are stars themselves!
